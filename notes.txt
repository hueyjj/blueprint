Target platform:
    Linux, Windows, Electron-compatible

Purpose:
    A multi-task tool to help virtually track and document the current state 
    of the user. 
    Ultimately, the tool is to reflect the absolute distillation 
    of the user's abilities, provide a context to construct improvements, 
    and give an informal, informational insight of the user.

Features:
    [Skill set] Map out a skill tree of current skills
        -Individual sections

    [Thoughts] Branch thoughts with logical paths

    [Guide] Goal 

    [Default routine]

    [Cards]

Light-weight, sleek design something like -> https://github.com/clangen/musikcube
